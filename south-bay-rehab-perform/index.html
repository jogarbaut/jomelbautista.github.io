About Dr. Takashi Kumagai Huynh, D.C.

His motivation for healthcare stems from an extensive personal struggle against his own body during his adolescence. In his youth, he visited countless doctors and underwent various tests, where unfortunately none of the doctors were able to specify what was going on. After years of tests, one doctor was able to diagnose him with a rare genetic disorder in fifteen short minutes just by listening to his symptoms. From there on, he knew that he wanted to instill that same relief to his patients. In his free time, he loves being active by playing basketball and running with friends. He also enjoys cooking new recipes to eat… which drives him to keep being active!

Clinic Services & Scheduler

Dr. Huynh separates himself from other chiropractors by figuring out the root cause of the pain and giving corrective exercises along with mobility training and load to address any issue. He believes that soft tissue work and adjustments are great and necessary but fixing the movement pattern issue is what he believes will have a lasting affect.

New Patients Eval & Treatment (First Visit)

Recommended for first time patients. A thorough history of the chief complaint and other health history screening will be done. Followed by some sort of movement assessment, breath work, soft tissue (IASTM and cupping if needed), adjustment, and corrective exercise.
