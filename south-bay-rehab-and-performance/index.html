Dr. Takashi Kumagai Huynh, D.C.

About the Doctor

Dr. Takashi Kumagai Huynh, D.C. is a proud Bay Area native!
His motivation for healthcare stems from an extensive personal struggle against his own body during his adolescence. In his youth, he visited countless doctors and underwent various tests, where unfortunately none of the doctors were able to specify what was going on. After years of tests, one doctor was able to diagnose him with a rare genetic disorder in fifteen short minutes just by listening to his symptoms. From there on, he knew that he wanted to instill that same relief to his patients. In his free time, he loves being active by playing basketball and running with friends. He also enjoys cooking new recipes to eat… which drives him to keep being active!

Doctor's Approach

The Doctor separates himself from other chiropractors by figuring out the root cause of the pain and giving corrective exercises along with mobility training and load to address any issue. He believes that soft tissue work and adjustments are great and necessary but fixing the movement pattern issue is what he believes will have a lasting affect.

About Our Clinic

Patients & Doctor

Here at South Bay Rehab and Performance, we take pride in our patient-centered approach.

Dr. Takashi D.C. has worked with a vast variety of patients including professional athletes, top national high school recruits, computer desk workers, geriatrics, and much more.

Our Method

Whether the goal of the patient is to take their game to the next level or a parent wanting to play with their kids pain free, we can help!

We are different from other chiropractors because we take the time to observe movement patterns through functional movement assessments and address compensations.

Our Goal

The ultimate goal of ours at South Bay Rehab and Performance is to phase the doctor out by giving the patient the tools to build resilient bodies and live a pain free life.
