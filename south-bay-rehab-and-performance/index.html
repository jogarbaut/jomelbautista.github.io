About

Here at South Bay, we take pride in our patient centered approach! Dr. Takashi D.C. has worked with a vast variety of patients including professional athletes, top national high school recruits, computer desk workers, geriatrics, and more. Whether the goal of the patient is to take his or her game to the next level or wanting to play with his or her kids pain free, we can help! We are different from other chiropractors because we take the time to observe movement patterns through functional movement assessments and address compensations. The ultimate goal of ours at South Bay is to phase the doctor out by giving the patient the tools to build resilient bodies and live a pain free life. By appointment only!

Dr. Takashi Huynh

About the Doctor

Dr. Takashi Huynh is a proud Bay Area native! He has a Bachelor of Science in Kinesiology from San José State University and a Doctorate from Palmer Chiropractic College West. His motivation for healthcare stems from an extensive personal struggle against his own body during his adolescence. In his youth, he visited countless doctors and underwent various tests, where unfortunately none of the doctors were able to specify what was going on. After years of tests, one doctor was able to diagnose him with a rare genetic disorder in fifteen short minutes just by listening to his symptoms. From there on, he knew that he wanted to instill that same relief to his patients. Dr. Takashi separates himself from other chiropractors by figuring out the root cause of the pain and giving corrective exercises along with mobility training and load to address any issue. He believes that soft tissue work and adjustments are great and necessary but fixing the movement pattern issue is what he believes will have a lasting affect. In his free time, he loves being active by playing basketball and running with friends. He also enjoys cooking new recipes to eat… which drives him to keep being active!
